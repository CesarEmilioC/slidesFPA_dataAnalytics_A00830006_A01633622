The result charts (and EDA charts) live under `figures/` and are regenerated by three scripts:

- `figures/_generate_results_charts.py` — main result / EDA bar charts (`results_*`, `sentiment_uplift`, `feature_importance`, `sector_volatility`, `class_balance`, `eda_news_sentiment`).
- `figures/_generate_extra_charts.py` — honest-framing visuals: `auc_ceiling.png` (slide 24), `honest_baseline_bars.png` (slide 21), `transaction_cost_erosion.png` (slide 25).
- `figures/_generate_prediction_charts.py` — diagnostic plots derived from the trained `models_demo/*.joblib` and the chronological 80/20 test slice: `roc_sp500.png` and `confusion_matrix_sp500.png` (slide 17), `calibration_sp500.png` (slide 23), `cumulative_returns_sp500.png` (slide 26). Requires `joblib`, `scikit-learn`, `xgboost`.

Authoritative source numbers come from `../FPA_dataAnalytics_finalRepository/datasets_/technical_model_results.csv` and `..._technical_sentiment_score_model_results.csv` (the older `final_model_comparison.csv` is **not** authoritative — its SP500 + RF + Sentiment numbers disagree with the per-CSV pair and should not be cited).

## Notes on the design
- Aspect ratio **16:9** for modern projectors.
- Tec de Monterrey colour palette (deep blue + warm gold), Open Sans typography.
- Margins fixed at 0.9 cm left / right; frame title sits in a coloured band so body content never bleeds off-slide.
- Footer carries authors, short title, and slide number — no navigation symbols.
- Title and thank-you slides are full-bleed with TikZ overlays; everything else uses the standard layout for readability.
